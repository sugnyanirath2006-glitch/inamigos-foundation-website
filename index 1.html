Who We Are

A grassroots network, built on collective action.

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, and is headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. What began as a small circle of volunteers has grown into a movement reaching underserved communities across multiple Indian states.

We believe lasting change happens at ground level — through food security, quality education, women's economic independence, skill development, animal welfare, and environmental care. Every initiative is run transparently, with a growing team of dedicated volunteers and corporate partners standing behind it.

Our Initiatives

Six projects, one thread of care.

Like a garland strung together, each InAmigos project addresses a distinct gap in society — together they form a complete ecosystem of support.

Project Seva

Food & Clothing

Distributing meals and clothing to underprivileged families — over 50,000 meals served to date.

Project Bachpanshala

Children's Education

Bridging learning gaps through digital literacy, life skills, and school support for underprivileged children.

Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Skill development and financial literacy programs that build a path toward independence.

Project Prakriti

Environment

Conservation drives, plantation efforts, and sustainability campaigns for a greener future.

Project Vikas

Skill & Employability

Vocational training programs that improve employability for youth entering the workforce.

Project Jeev

Animal Welfare

Care, rescue, and welfare initiatives extending compassion to animals in our communities.
